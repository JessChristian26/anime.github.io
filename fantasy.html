The Best Anime of Every Genre

Looking for the best anime per genre? This is your guide to the best anime shows in almost every genre? These popular shows defined
their genre to the point where their influence radiates out throughout all of anime today.

Action, Magic - October 28, 2020

The Most Popular Anime: Fantasy

Facebook Pinterest Linkedin Twitter

Fantasy as a genre is very loosely defined and can often encompass many different shows. Generally
speaking, these anime tend to be set in unique worlds or a variation of the real world with fantastical
elements. Fantasy anime vary a lot and has may sub-types that one can choose to watch.

One Punch Man 8.8/10

Superheroes are meant to be brave, strong and upstanding citizens of their great nation. Saitama is only
one of these things – strong. In fact, he’s so strong that just one punch from him can send the strongest
people flying through the sky.

This is why he doesn’t take his superhero life seriously. In his own words, he’s a superhero for fun. This anime
revolves around the hilarious antics of Saitama the hero, as well as Saitama the human being.

March Comes In Like A Lion: 8.4/10

Few shows better capture the bleakness of depression than March Comes In Like A Lion. The main character
Rei Kiriyama is a reclusive and isolated person who avoids seeing others. Despite being a professional shogi
player, Rei lives a listless life - one that is shaken up by the Kawamoto Sisters when they welcome him into
their warm family environment. This show's slow pace and careful character study make for an enthralling
slice of life that leans more on the drama than the comedy. All of this is supplemented by a distinctive art
style that draws viewers in.

Attack on Titan: 8.8/10

Monstrous creatures known as titans have nearly eliminated the human races. Survivors live behind
extremely tall and sturdy walls, which the titans can’t penetrate. Without a single titan sighting for a century,
everybody is shocked when a titan breaches those very walls.

A young Eren witnesses the horrifying sights of titan destroying his village and killing people, including his
mother. After the aftermath has ended, he and his friends Mikasa and Armin pledge to join the military unit
in order to kill as many titans as they can.

Hunter X Hunter: 8.9/10

Living in a world where Hunters exist, young Gon unexpectedly learns a hard truth – that his father
had abandoned him in order to pursue his passion as a Hunter. Shocked and hurt, the young boy is
determined to find his father and ask him face to face why he made such a harsh decision.

So begins Gon’s journey as a Hunter himself. On his way, he meets new friends, makes a few enemies, faces
many hardships but most importantly, learns the true meaning of being a hunter.

Death Note: 9.0/10

Light Yagami is an extremely intelligent schoolboy who’s bored out of his wits. However, the unimaginable
happens one day when a shinigami gives him a book called the Death Note. Through that book, he could kill
anyone he wanted without being caught.

Thus, Light goes on a killing spree. But it doesn’t take the authorities long to realize what is happening, which
is when detective L is recruited. With L being as intelligent as Light, will the latter ever be caught and
punished for his crimes?
